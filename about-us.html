About us

A brief intro about us

Our team started this website for the REM hackathon that is taking place in Bright Riders School and we have worked very hard and used multiple websites for help, insipration and have created this website to show and transport the audience through various key moments in time.

What this website is

This website consists of the most remarkable, major and important moments in the history of space travel. Milestones such as the first time we ventured out of space, the first man to step on the moon, etc all the way up to the latest major milestones that are taking place in the conquest to explore space.
